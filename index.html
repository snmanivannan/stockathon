Frequently Asked Questions

Is Stocksathon real trading?

No. Stocksathon is a stock trading game. You pick real stocks and track real market prices, but no real money is involved. You cannot buy or sell actual stocks through this app.

Is Stocksathon free?

Yes. Stocksathon is completely free to download and use. There are no in-app purchases required to participate in challenges.

Is real money involved?

No. Stocksathon is a simulation game. You cannot deposit, withdraw, or trade with real money. Your portfolio returns are virtual.

Can beginners use Stocksathon?

Yes. Stocksathon is designed for beginners. You don't need any trading experience. Just pick stocks you think will go up and see how you do.

Is Stocksathon available in India and the US?

Yes. Stocksathon is available worldwide on Google Play. The app tracks US market stocks, so users in any country can participate.

How do challenges work?

Each challenge has a start date and end date. Before the start date, you pick 5 stocks. When the challenge starts, your picks are locked and you compete against other players based on portfolio returns.
